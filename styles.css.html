Primary Services

Home Assessments: Nationwide Expertise

No matter where you call home across the United States, our comprehensive assessment services are available to you through cutting-edge LiDAR scanning technology that virtually brings your house to our expert team. We import the scan into our specialized design software for thorough analysis, delivering prompt and cost-effective evaluations without compromising on accuracy. After the 3D model is created, we collaborate with all stakeholders to develop a customized home evaluation explicitly tailored to your needs and circumstances.

Accessible Home Design – Transforming Spaces

Accessible remodeling is personal—there’s no one-size-fits-all code, so we design around you. Using LiDAR scanning, we create a precise digital model of your home and collaborate with you to develop layouts that prioritize safety, independence, and dignity. With over 1,000 custom accessibility solutions designed since 2007, we bring both experience and innovation to every project. Contractors appreciate our clear, well-documented plans because great design leads to confident construction.

Equipment Solutions: Supporting Complex Care at Home

Caring for children and adults with complex medical conditions becomes more manageable with specialized equipment for positioning, skin protection, transfers, and bathing—including specialty beds, patient lifts, and shower wheelchairs. Our comprehensive solutions extend to rehabilitation tools like standers and gait trainers, as well as accessibility equipment such as lifts and elevators that transform daily living. Our occupational therapists and Assistive Technology Professionals collaborate closely with caregivers and vendors to identify the perfect equipment solutions tailored to your unique needs. We guide you through the entire process, from assessment to obtaining multiple estimates, ensuring you receive the optimal equipment to enhance independence and quality of care.

Real Estate – Wheelchair Accessible Housing Solutions

Finding wheelchair-accessible housing is a nationwide challenge, as privately owned homes rarely meet accessibility codes and apartment communities often lack fully accessible units despite compliant common areas. With two decades of expertise, we guide you through this complex process with precision and care. Our team identifies the most suitable housing options that require minimal renovations to meet your unique accessibility needs. Trust us to deliver tailored solutions that enhance independence and comfort.

Expert Opinion Reports

Since 2010, we've provided authoritative expert opinion reports on wheelchair accessible housing to both plaintiff and defense counsel with unwavering objectivity. Our comprehensive assessments consistently deliver recommendations grounded in verifiable data sources rather than subjective interpretations. When engaged as experts, we maintain the same rigorous standards regardless of which party retains our services, ensuring our findings remain credible, defensible, and focused on reasonable accommodations that serve real accessibility needs.

About Us

Accessibility Architects: Where Expertise Meets Empathy

Since 1998, we've been navigating the complex landscape of residential accessibility solutions, formalizing our expertise under Accessible Housing Services in 2007. Our portfolio includes high-profile, publicly known cases involving catastrophically injured individuals, where our unique strength lies in harmonizing competing interests—balancing homeowners' desires for quality upgrades against payers' budgetary constraints—all while staying grounded in what's objectively reasonable. This delicate balancing act has made us the trusted mediator in countless accessibility projects, even those entangled in legal proceedings.

Our multidisciplinary team of healthcare professionals, construction specialists, and real estate experts brings a holistic approach to every project, ensuring comprehensive solutions that respect both practical needs and fiscal realities. Whether you're a legal professional navigating an accessibility dispute, a healthcare provider seeking housing solutions for clients, or a homeowner embarking on accessibility modifications, our two decades of specialized experience ensures your project receives expert guidance built on objectivity rather than bias. While some consultants might tell you what you want to hear, we commit to something more valuable—what you need to know. This unwavering commitment to integrity has earned us recognition as court-qualified experts in wheelchair-accessible housing, making us the partner of choice for those who value both competence and candor.
